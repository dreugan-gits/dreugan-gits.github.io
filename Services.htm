Unfortunately, new virii and malware come out at an astounding rate. Guardian I.T. Services will scan your system with the latest tools in virus, malware, spyware and adware detection. Guardian I.T. Services also has experience doing manual removal of suspected software and components even before new virus or malware definitions are out. Wireless Devices/WiFi
Do you have a new wireless laptop that you really want to be able to use on your backyard deck? Do you have a spot in your house you want to use a desktop/laptop, but don't want messy cables running everywhere? Or do you already have wireless setup and want to make sure it's secure so your neighbors aren't stealing your bandwidth? 
 
Wireless networking or WiFi has progressed and become more widely available in the last few years. With the popularity of WiFi, the concept of "wardriving" has increased. "WarDriving" is when a person drives around trying to find unprotected wireless systems, either to hack them or to use them for free internet. Let Guardian I.T. Services setup and secure your wireless network from intruders. We have experience in setting up and securing wireless access points, wireless routers, and wireless network cards for both the home user and the corporate application.
